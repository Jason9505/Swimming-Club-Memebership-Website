# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Swimming Club Attendance Tracking System

---

# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

Swimming Club Attendance Tracking System

---

## Purpose

A web-based attendance and membership tracking system for a swimming club. Members record attendance using their Student ID, and the system automatically generates a digital membership card after successful attendance submission.

The system integrates with Google Sheets as the database and automatically validates membership status based on registration date.

---

## Goals

* Simplify attendance taking
* Reduce manual administrative work
* Automatically validate memberships
* Generate digital membership cards
* Provide attendance reporting tools for admins
* Use lightweight infrastructure with Google Sheets

---

# 2. Background & Problem Statement

Current attendance management is manual and inefficient, causing:

* Slow attendance registration
* Human recording errors
* Difficulty tracking active memberships
* Difficulty generating reports

The proposed system digitizes attendance and membership verification while keeping infrastructure simple and low-cost.

---

# 5. Functional Requirements

# 5.1 Member Attendance Check-In

## Description

Members record attendance by entering their Student ID into the attendance website.

After successful submission, the system displays the member’s digital swimming club membership card.

---

## Attendance Workflow

1. Member opens attendance page
2. Member enters Student ID
3. System searches Google Sheets database
4. System validates membership
5. Attendance is recorded
6. Digital membership card is generated and displayed

# 5.2 Digital Membership Card

## Description

After successful attendance submission, the system generates and displays a digital swimming club membership card.

The card acts as:

* Membership verification
* Attendance confirmation
* Member identity display

---

## Card Design Requirements

The digital card should visually resemble a modern membership card with:

* Metallic/dark themed background
* Member ID displayed prominently
* Membership dates
* Membership type indicator
* Club branding

---

## Card Information Displayed

| Field             | Source                |
| ----------------- | --------------------- |
| Student ID        | Google Sheets         |
| Full Name         | Google Sheets         |
| Member Since      | Start time            |
| Valid Thru        | Start time + 1 year   |
| Membership Status | Active / Expired      |
| Swimming Level    | Swimming Level column |

---

## Swimming Level Display

The membership level section on the card displays the member’s swimming level from Google Sheets.

Examples:

* Beginner
* Intermediate
* Advanced

---

## Example Logic

If Google Sheets contains:

| Student ID | Swimming Level |
| ---------- | -------------- |
| 252UC2546J | Beginner       |

Then the digital card displays:

```text id="82d4p4"
BEGINNER
```

---

## Membership Card Conditions

### Active Membership

If membership is valid:

* Digital card is displayed normally

---

### Expired Membership

If membership expired:

* Card displays:

```text id="kg8trw"
Membership Expired
```

* Card may visually indicate expired status

# 5.3 Membership Expiration Logic

## Registration Date Source

The system uses:

```text id="k78dbf"
Start time
```

from Google Form responses as the official registration date.

---

## Expiry Calculation

```text id="wq0wq5"
Membership Expiry Date = Start time + 1 Year
```

---

## Membership Conditions

### Active Membership

Condition:

```text id="0r87a2"
Current Date <= Expiry Date
```

Display:

```text id="4xg0nb"
Membership Active
```

---

### Expired Membership

Condition:

```text id="zy5cv8"
Current Date > Expiry Date
```

Display:

```text id="p3vwfw"
Membership Expired
```

---

### Step 3


---

### Step 4

Validate membership status

---

### Step 5

Check duplicate attendance for current day

---

### Step 6

Record attendance into Attendance Sheet

### Step 7

Generate digital membership card
